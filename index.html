About Us

Welcome to Siquijor– The Island of Enchantment

Nestled in the heart of the Visayas, Siquijor is a tropical paradise known for its pristine beaches, majestic waterfalls, and mystical charm. Whether you seek adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, our island offers an unforgettable experience.

Explore hidden caves, dive into crystal-clear waters, and witness breathtaking sunsets that paint the sky. Beyond its natural beauty, Siquijor is home to rich traditions, warm locals, and a fascinating heritage of healing rituals and folklore.

Searvices

Our Services

Island Tours & Sightseeing

Explore Siquijor’s best spots, like Cambugahay Falls, Salagdoong Beach, and the Balete Tree. Guided tours ensure a hassle-free experience.

Snorkeling & Scuba Diving

Dive into clear waters and see colorful corals, turtles, and fish. Visit Tulapos Marine Sanctuary and Paliton Beach.

Motorbike & Bicycle Rentals

Rent a motorbike or bicycle to explore beaches, waterfalls, and scenic roads at your own pace.

Healing & Wellness Retreats

Experience hilot massages, herbal healing, and spiritual cleansing. Siquijor is known for its traditional healers.

Beachfront Resorts & Homestays

Stay in luxury resorts or cozy homestays with ocean views and local hospitality for a relaxing getaway.

Island Hopping & Boat Tours

Sail to nearby islands like Apo Island or Sumilon Island. Visit hidden beaches, sandbars, and caves.

Firefly Watching & Night Tours

Experience the magical glow of fireflies at night in mangrove areas, and stargaze on Siquijor’s peaceful, unpolluted beaches.

Local Food & Culinary Tours

Taste fresh seafood and local delicacies like Torta and Pan Bisaya on a guided food tour.
